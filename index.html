An interactive experiment with p5.play

ℹ️ About the project
This project is a personal exploration of the p5.play library, built on top of p5.js. It simulates a small digital fish tank where each fish is an independent sprite with its own movement and behavior.
Every time the page is refreshed, the tank is repopulated with five new fish, each generated with random colors, sizes, and initial positions, making every visit slightly different.

Hover interaction
Hovering over a fish makes it pause for a moment, as if startled or curious, before it swims away again with a new speed and direction.

Click interaction
Clicking anywhere in the tank drops a small food flake, and the fish immediately react by moving toward it to eat.

Why this experiment
The project is a playful way to experiment with sprite behavior, motion dynamics, randomness, and user interaction in creative coding, a simple scene that becomes surprisingly alive through a few lines of logic.
